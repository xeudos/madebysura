Music Composer & Sound Designer

I'm Daniel, an Amsterdam-based music producer working under the name Sura (a playful nod to my last name - Sura means "calf" in Latin, Kuit means "calf" in Dutch). I create original music and sound design for video, ads, and media projects.

My background combines Media Entertainment Management with 10 years of hands-on music production experience, plus I work with a solid network of musicians and creatives. This means I can help you nail the right vibe for your project.

Got a clear idea of what you want sonically? Great, let's build it. Still figuring it out? Even better! I love that early exploration phase. Either way, I'm here to make your project sound memorable.
