Daniel Kuiters // Sura

Music Composer & Sound Designer

I create original music and sound design for video, ads, and media projects under the name Sura, a playful reference to my Dutch surname (Kuit means "calf," Sura means the same in Latin).

With a background in Media Entertainment Management and 10 years of hands-on production experience, I work with a trusted network of musicians and creatives to deliver the right sonic identity for your project.

Whether you have a clear vision or you're still exploring possibilities, I'm here to collaborate and make your project sound memorable.
